Taskmaster Spreadsheet Formulas


This formula will sum all the values in column M on the "tasks" sheet where the corresponding value in column B matches the value in cell B2.


‭=SUMIF(tasks!B:B, B2, tasks!M:M)‬


tasks!B:B: This is the range where you're checking for the condition (i.e., column B on the "tasks" sheet).
B2: This is the value you're looking for in column B on the "tasks" sheet.
tasks!M:M: This is the range of values you want to sum (i.e., column M on the "tasks" sheet) where the condition is met.
